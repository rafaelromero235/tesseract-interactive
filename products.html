Mystical Tactics

Mystical Tactics is a combat card game with augmented reality that combines strategy, magic, and real-time tactics. Players collect and customize a deck of mystical cards, each representing heroes, spells, and creatures with unique abilities. Using augmented reality, battles come to life in physical environments, projecting spectacular visual effects and 3D creatures onto the game table or any surface. In Mystical Tactics, the key to success lies in strategic planning and mastering card synergies. Players can summon creatures, cast spells, and activate special abilities to outmaneuver their opponents. With a magical aesthetic and an immersive gameplay experience enhanced by augmented reality, Mystical Tactics offers an innovative experience for card game and strategy enthusiasts.
